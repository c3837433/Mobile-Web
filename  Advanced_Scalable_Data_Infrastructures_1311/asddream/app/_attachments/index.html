About In My Dreams

There are hundreds of books and resources on the market today to help people understand and interpret their dreams. The truth about dreams though, is that the meaning of the dream is usually found in the dreamer. When reflecting over a dream, try to give it a simple title. This can give the dream perspective. Each dream may have things, people, or events that stick out to you, think about these things in terms of what they mean to you. Over time the dreams begin to unravel.
