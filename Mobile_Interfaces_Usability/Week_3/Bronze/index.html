About Me

Welcome!

Life is an amazing journey. Even as little as five years ago I would never have thought that I would be designing and creating mobile applications. Well, let's get real. Looking back just a decade ago the market was not nearly the same as we find it today.

A few years ago while watching a DVD bonus video, I listened to someone begin to list off all my skills. Enjoy working with spreadsheets, love researching, can pour hours into a project and then let it go, and others. It was then that the idea of using the gifts I have to create things began to emerge.

Now here I am, continuing on this amazing journey, and you get to play around on my first app. :)

Thanks for stopping by,

Angela
